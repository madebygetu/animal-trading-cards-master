Ethiopian Wolf

Ethiopian Wolf is one of the rarest carnivores on the planet. Fewer than 500 remain.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Simensis
  • Avarage Length: 33-40 in
  • Avarage Lifespan: 8-10 years
  • Habitat: Alpine regions of Ethiopia

Ethiopian wolves are the rarest of all canids. In fact, they are one of the rarest mammals worldwide,and are endangered. Ethiopian wolves are believed to have evolved from grey wolves that migrated from Eurasia to Northern Africa around 100,000 years ago. They are closely related to domestic dogs, coyotes and grey wolves. Their habitat lies in a remote part of Ethiopia known as the Afroalpine highlands.
